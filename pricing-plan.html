Common Questions

Do I need to be home for service?

No! As long as our crew has access to the yard (gates unlocked), we can complete the work and will send you a confirmation when finished.

What areas do you service?

We primarily serve Georgetown, GTA and all surrounding areas. Contact us if you are unsure if you are within our range.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your monthly plan at any time by contacting our office.

How does billing work?

We bill monthly for our maintenance plans. You can pay securely online via credit card or bank transfer.
