What's Included in Every Visit?

We don't just cut the grass and leave. Our standard visit includes a comprehensive 4-step process to ensure your property looks its absolute best.

1. Precision Mowing

We cut your grass at the optimal height for the season. We also alternate our mowing patterns weekly to prevent ruts and encourage upright, healthy growth.

2. Detailed Trimming

We carefully line-trim around fences, trees, garden beds, mailboxes, and any other obstacles where our standard mowers cannot safely reach.

3. Crisp Edging

We vertically edge along all concrete surfaces—including your driveway, sidewalks, and patios—to give your lawn a sharp, distinct, and clean border.

4. Complete Clean-up

We finish every single visit by blowing all grass clippings off of your hard surfaces, ensuring your property is left spotless and ready to enjoy.
