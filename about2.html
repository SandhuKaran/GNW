About Greenworks

Expert turf management and reliable property care across Georgetown and the Greater Toronto Area.

More Than Just a Quick Cut

Precision isn’t just a goal. Perfecting your lawn is our passion.

Greenworks Landscaping Inc. is a full-service landscaping company committed to providing top-quality lawn care and construction services. Locally owned and operated in Georgetown, ON, we pride ourselves on delivering reliable and personalized care tailored to each property's unique needs.

From weekly mowing and specialized fertilization to heavy hardscaping, sodding, and commercial maintenance contracts, our experienced team ensures that every job is done right. We treat every property with the same dedication as our own, ensuring you can stop worrying about maintenance and start enjoying your outdoor space.

You Can Count on Us

We know how frustrating it is when a lawn care company doesn't show up. At Greenworks, our schedule is our word. When we set a day for your weekly or bi-weekly service, our crew will be there. We utilize route optimization software to ensure consistent timing and communication with all our clients.

We are available for on-site quoting starting the first week of March, and we begin taking bookings for our season (April 15th to November 15th) in the first week of April.

Maintained for Perfection

A great cut requires a sharp blade. We run top-of-the-line commercial mowers, trimmers, and edgers. More importantly, we meticulously maintain our equipment, sharpening mower blades daily to ensure a clean cut that doesn't tear or damage your grass, promoting a healthier, greener lawn.

Trusted by Businesses

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond residential properties. Greenworks Landscaping is trusted to maintain high-traffic commercial accounts, including multiple Flying J locations across Etobicoke, Mississauga, and London. We have the manpower and the heavy-duty equipment to handle large-scale contracts reliably.

Fully Licensed & Insured

Your property is safe in our hands. Greenworks Landscaping Inc. is fully licensed, insured, and WSIB compliant. We believe in complete transparency and building trust with our community. If you are ever unsatisfied with our service, let us know, and we will return to make it right—no questions asked.
