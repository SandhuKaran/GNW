The Installation Process

We don't cut corners. Here is how we guarantee a long-lasting surface.

1

Excavation

We dig deep (12-18 inches) to remove unstable soil and ensure a solid foundation.

2

Base Prep

Layers of gravel are compacted in stages to create a rock-hard, non-shifting base.

3

Laying

Pavers are hand-laid in your chosen pattern with precise cuts and tight joints.

4

Finishing

We apply polymeric sand to lock joints and prevent weeds, leaving a pristine finish.
