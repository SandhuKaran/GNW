The Science Beneath

A wall is only as good as its foundation. Here is how we ensure longevity.

Deep Foundation

We excavate below the frost line and install a compacted gravel base to prevent heaving.

Proper Drainage

Every wall includes clear stone backfill and weeping tiles to relieve hydrostatic pressure.

Geogrid Reinforcement

For taller walls, we use structural geogrid mesh to anchor the wall into the slope.
