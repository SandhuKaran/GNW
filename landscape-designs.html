Our Design Process

From initial concept to final construction, we guide you every step of the way.

1

Consultation

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer 3D designs?

Yes, we specialize in 3D landscape rendering. This helps you visualize the finished project, including materials, lighting, and planting, ensuring you are 100% happy before construction starts.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Georgetown, Halton Hills, Brampton, Mississauga, and surrounding areas in the GTA.

How long does the design process take?

Typically, the design phase takes 1-2 weeks depending on the complexity of the project and the speed of feedback. We work with you to refine the concept until it's perfect.

Do you handle permits?

Yes, we can assist with obtaining necessary municipal permits for projects involving structural work, zoning, or conservation areas.
